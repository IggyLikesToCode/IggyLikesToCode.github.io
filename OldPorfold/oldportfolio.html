Neurodiversity Hub

  • Our Goal - We, Akademeia students, are partnered with the Max and Mary foundation, to raise awareness for autism and help others understand the world from the lens of someone who is neurodivergent. Thanks to the outreach of our school, we want to provide funding for the construction of a library at the Max & Mary headquarters.

  • Events - To raise funding for our cause, we are organizing a Neurodiversity Week at our school, filled with events such as a 3v3 basketball tournament and a Rose Sale for Valentines Day. We hope that by the end of the week, we may educate our peers about what it means to be neurodivergent, thanks to the workshops which we will be running throughout this period.
